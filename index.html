Outside Time — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Outside Time ("we," "us," "the app") helps parents and caregivers find nearby outdoor places and activities for kids. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Outside Time, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Your password is encrypted and never stored or visible to us in plain text — authentication is handled by our backend provider, Supabase.

Profile information (optional). You may choose to add a display name, a profile photo, your children's birth month and year (used only to show age-appropriate recommendations — we do not collect your children's names or other identifying details), and your home city.

Location information. With your permission, we use your device's location while you're using the app to show you nearby places and activities. We only access your location in the foreground, while the app is open — we do not track or store a history of your location over time. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings; the app still works, but you'll need to search by city or area manually.

Photos and user content. If you add a photo, submit a new place, suggest an activity, or report an issue with a listing, we collect the content you submit, including any images. Photos and submissions may be shown publicly within the app to other users, since they're meant to help other families.

Automatically collected information. Our backend infrastructure (Supabase) may log standard technical information, such as IP address and request timestamps, for security and abuse-prevention purposes. We do not currently use any third-party analytics or advertising services within the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core features: showing nearby places and activities, personalizing results for your children's ages, and letting you save favorites.
  • To support community contributions: displaying photos, place/activity submissions, and alerts submitted by users.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the app and prevent abuse.
  • To communicate with you about your account, if necessary.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as follows:

  • Service providers. We use Supabase, Inc. to host our database, authentication, and file storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf.
  • Map providers. Map display is provided by Apple Maps (iOS) or Google Maps (Android), which may process location and map-tile requests under their own respective privacy policies.
  • Public content. Any photo, place submission, activity submission, or alert you choose to submit may be visible to other users of the app.
  • Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Children's Privacy

Outside Time is designed for use by parents and caregivers, not by children directly. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Any information about your children (limited to birth month/year) is provided voluntarily by you, the parent or caregiver, and is used only to personalize recommendations.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and profile information for as long as your account is active. You can update or remove most profile information directly within the app. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at stephsjlee@gmail.com — we'll process deletion requests promptly.

Your Choices

  • Location. Allow or deny location access in your device settings at any time.
  • Photos. Allow or deny photo library access in your device settings at any time.
  • Profile data. Edit or remove most of your profile information from within the app.
  • Account deletion. Contact us as described above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at stephsjlee@gmail.com.
